Sunset & Wine Cruise

Set sail on a serene evening cruise, where you can relax, sip your favorite wine, and enjoy Charleston's stunning coastline as the sun sets over the water. It's the perfect way to unwind and take in the beauty of the Lowcountry in a tranquil setting.

Sea Shell & Shark Tooth Hunt

Join us for a fun and exciting treasure hunt along the local beaches! Collect unique seashells and hunt for ancient shark teeth as you explore the shores of Charleston's islands and sandbars. It's a family-friendly adventure with something for everyone.

Island & Sandbar Hoping

Cruise through the beautiful waterways of Charleston, hopping from one island to the next and discovering secluded sandbars along the way. Whether you want to swim, sunbathe, or just explore, this tour is all about fun in the sun and adventure on the water.

Charleston Historic Harbor Tour

Step back in time and discover Charleston's rich history on a scenic harbor tour. Learn about the city's iconic landmarks, historic sites, and naval history while cruising past forts, plantations, and stunning waterfront views.

Swim & Sunbathing Fun

Drop anchor in one of Charleston's beautiful, calm waters for a relaxing day of swimming and sunbathing. Perfect for those looking to cool off and enjoy some time in the sun, this tour offers the ultimate laid-back experience.

Lunch or Dinner at a Waterfront Restaurant

Take a break from cruising and enjoy a delicious meal at one of Charleston's waterfront restaurants. Whether you're craving fresh seafood or a leisurely lunch, we'll dock at a scenic spot so you can enjoy the best food and views Charleston has to offer.
